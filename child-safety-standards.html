Our Commitment to Child Safety
Weino has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to creating a safe environment for all users and take immediate action against any content or behavior that threatens the safety of minors.

1. Age Restrictions and Verification

Weino is designed for users aged 13 and above. We implement the following measures:

  • Age Requirement: Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account, in compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).
  • Account Creation: During registration, users must confirm they meet the minimum age requirement.
  • Reporting Underage Users: We encourage users to report any accounts they suspect belong to users under 13 years of age.
  • Immediate Action: Any account found to belong to a user under 13 will be immediately suspended and deleted.

2. Prevention of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

Weino actively prevents the creation, distribution, or sharing of CSAM through:

  • Content Moderation: Automated systems and manual review processes to detect and remove inappropriate content.
  • User Reporting: In-app reporting mechanisms that allow users to flag concerning content or behavior immediately.
  • Proactive Monitoring: Continuous monitoring of user-generated content, including profile pictures, messages, and shared media.
  • Zero Tolerance Policy: Immediate and permanent ban of any user found to be sharing, creating, or distributing CSAM.
  • Technology Solutions: Use of industry-standard tools and technologies to detect and prevent the upload of harmful content.

3. Reporting Mechanisms

Weino provides multiple ways for users to report child safety concerns:

  • In-App Reporting: Users can report concerning content, behavior, or accounts directly through the app's reporting feature.
  • Email Reporting: Direct email reporting to our safety team (see contact information below).
  • Priority Processing: All child safety reports are processed with the highest priority and urgency.
  • Confidentiality: Reports are handled with strict confidentiality to protect the safety of all parties involved.

4. Response and Action Procedures

When a child safety concern is reported, Weino takes immediate action:

  • Immediate Review: All child safety reports are reviewed within 24 hours, with critical cases reviewed immediately.
  • Account Suspension: Accounts under investigation for child safety violations are immediately suspended pending review.
  • Content Removal: Any content identified as CSAM or harmful to minors is immediately removed from our platform.
  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and report suspected CSAM to the appropriate authorities, including:
    • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) in the United States
    • Relevant national authorities in other jurisdictions
    • International law enforcement agencies as appropriate
  • Permanent Bans: Users found to have violated child safety standards are permanently banned from the platform.

5. Legal Compliance

Weino complies with all relevant child safety laws and regulations, including:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act): Compliance with U.S. federal law protecting children's privacy online.
  • Regional Laws: Compliance with child protection laws in all jurisdictions where Weino operates.
  • International Standards: Adherence to international best practices for child online safety.
  • Mandatory Reporting: We report suspected CSAM to relevant regional and national authorities as required by law.

6. User Education and Awareness

Weino promotes child safety awareness through:

  • Safety Guidelines: Clear safety guidelines provided to all users during account creation and in-app.
  • Community Standards: Published community standards that explicitly prohibit any behavior harmful to minors.
  • Educational Resources: Links to educational resources about online safety for users and parents.
  • Regular Updates: Ongoing communication about safety features and reporting mechanisms.

7. Technology and Safety Infrastructure

Weino invests in technology and infrastructure to protect child safety:

  • Automated Detection: Advanced automated systems to detect and flag potentially harmful content.
  • Human Moderation: Trained human moderators who review reported content and user behavior.
  • Data Retention: Appropriate data retention policies that preserve evidence for law enforcement while protecting user privacy.
  • Secure Storage: Secure storage of reported content and evidence in compliance with legal requirements.

9. Continuous Improvement

Weino is committed to continuously improving our child safety measures:

  • Regular Review: We regularly review and update our child safety policies and procedures.
  • Industry Collaboration: We collaborate with industry partners, safety organizations, and law enforcement to stay current with best practices.
  • Technology Updates: We continuously update our technology and tools to better detect and prevent child safety violations.
  • User Feedback: We welcome feedback from users, parents, and safety organizations to improve our safety measures.
