⚠️ Permanent Action
Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

How to Request Account Deletion

1
Open the MatchyTo App

Launch the MatchyTo app on your Android device and make sure you are logged in.

2
Navigate to Settings

Go to the Profile tab (bottom navigation) and tap on "Settings" or the settings icon.

3
Select Delete Account

Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" option and tap on it.

4
Confirm Deletion

Read the confirmation message carefully, then confirm that you want to permanently delete your account.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your MatchyTo account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password, and authentication credentials
  • Profile Data: Profile picture and all profile settings
  • Calendar Data: All your availability slots and calendar information
  • Social Connections: All friend connections, friend requests (sent and received)
  • Matches: All matches (pending, accepted, and expired) you created or participated in
  • Messages: All messages and chat conversations in match threads
  • Notifications: All notification history
  • Preferences: Language, country, timezone, and all app settings
  • Push Notification Tokens: FCM tokens for push notifications
  • Premium Subscriptions: Any active premium subscriptions will be cancelled (iOS only - not applicable for Android)

Data Retention Period

Immediate Deletion: Most of your data is deleted immediately when you request account deletion.

Backup Retention: Some information may remain in our backup systems for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes. After 90 days, all backup copies are permanently deleted.

Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymized analytics data (which cannot identify you) may be retained longer for service improvement purposes.

What Happens After Deletion

  • You will be immediately logged out of the app
  • You will no longer receive any notifications from MatchyTo
  • Your username will become available for other users
  • You will need to create a new account if you want to use MatchyTo again in the future
  • You cannot recover any deleted data

Alternative: Contact Support

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, or if you have questions about account deletion, you can contact us:

  • Email: support@matchyto.com
  • Support Page: Visit our Support page

When contacting us, please include your username or email address so we can locate your account.

Need Help?
If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, email us at support@matchyto.com. We're here to help.
